# 01 Project Overview

## System Name
Hospital Management System (HMS)

## Mission Statement
To provide a centralized, organized, and efficient web-based platform for hospitals to manage daily operations, digitize medical processes, and enhance the quality of healthcare services.

## Core Objective
- **Efficiency**: Reduce paperwork and manual work.
- **Accessibility**: Provide easy access to medical histories and treatment records.
- **Coordination**: Streamline interactions between Patients, Doctors, and Administrators.
- **Security**: Ensure patient data is organized, secure, and accessible only to authorized roles.

## Target Audience
- **Patients**: Seeking medical care and management of personal health records.
- **Doctors**: Managing patient care, diagnoses, and medical prescriptions.
- **Administrators**: Handling hospital resources, staff, and overall system operations.
