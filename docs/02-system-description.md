# 02 System Description

## System Nature
The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a multi-user Web Application designed to digitize and manage the lifecycle of hospital operations. It acts as a digital backbone for medical facilities, ensuring that every touchpoint—from patient registration to billing—is recorded and managed.

## Key Actors
1. **Administrators**: Power users who manage the infrastructure (doctors, staff, medications, billing).
2. **Doctors**: Primary care providers who manage clinical data (diagnoses, prescriptions, medical reports).
3. **Patients**: Service recipients who manage their own profiles, appointments, and view their medical history.

## High-Level Capabilities
- **Electronic Health Records (EHR)**: Secure storage of patient medical histories.
- **Scheduling**: Real-time appointment booking and doctor availability management.
- **Inventory Management**: Tracking medication stock and pharmacy data.
- **Financial Module**: Generation of invoices and tracking of payments.
- **Notification System**: Automated alerts for appointments and prescription updates.
