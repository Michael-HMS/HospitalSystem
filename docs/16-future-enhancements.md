# 16 Future Enhancements

## 1. AI Integration
- Predictive diagnostics based on historical patient medical data.
- Automated appointment categorization and prioritization.

## 2. Mobile Application
- Dedicated iOS/Android apps for patients to view records and book appointments on the go.

## 3. Telemedicine
- Integration of video conferencing for remote consultations.

## 4. IoT & Wearables
- Syncing data from patient wearable devices (heart rate, blood pressure) directly into medical records.

## 5. Multi-Hospital Support
- Supporting multi-tenant hospital chains with shared medical research data but isolated operational data.
